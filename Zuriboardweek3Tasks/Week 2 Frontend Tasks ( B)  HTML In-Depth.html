Tribute Page to Pastor Chris Oyakhilome (Ph.d, D.d, D.Sc)

This code is a tribute to Pastor Chris Oyakhilome ,my mentor, teacher and man of God. He taught me the word of God and has inspired me to live a life of victory and greatness. He's majorly the one who has inspired me to go towards the 'Tech' life
I fell in love with his Love for God, the Gospel and for people in generation, with His faith life, the excellence displayed in ministry, his tenacity and lastly his deep relationship with God.

Introduction

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome is an anointed man of God .He is the Founder and President of the Believers' Loveworld Nation Incorporated also known as Christ Embassy.His work and contribution to humanity is something worth applauding and recognizing.Pastor Chris was given an Honorary Doctorates from Ambrose Alli University and Benson Idahosa University

His Work

Pastor Chris is the founder of the global ministry known as Believers Loveworld nation -aka Christ Embassy.He oversees multiple ministries with a primary mission of taking God’s divine presence to the nations of the world and to demonstrate the character of the Holy Spirit, driven by a purpose to see men and women all over the world come to the knowledge of the divine life made available in Christ Jesus and committed to the mission of raising generations of men and women that will come into their inheritance as we teach the word of God, so that they will fulfill God’s plan for them.

"God has never made a failure; that he gave birth to you means you are a success. Success is in your DNA."
- Chris Oyakhilome,Rhapsody of Realities
